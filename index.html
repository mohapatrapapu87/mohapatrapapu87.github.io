Major GIS Projects

  • Renad Academy Building GIS Project (Qatar)
    Developed PWA-compliant building geodatabases, CAD to GIS conversion, feature class creation, attribute population, topology validation, QA/QC and GIS data submission.

  • Domestic Water Supply GIS Project (KAHRAMAA, Qatar)
    Managed water utility GIS database including water mains, valves, hydrants, meters and service connections. Performed CAD to GIS conversion, versioning, reconcile/post, topology validation and network connectivity checks.

  • Electricity Network GIS Project (KAHRAMAA, Qatar)
    Developed and maintained LV and MV electrical network GIS layers including transformers, substations, feeder pillars, cables and streetlights. Performed ArcFM editing, connectivity validation and enterprise GIS updates.

  • Planametry Road Network GIS Project (ASHGHAL, Qatar)
    Created and updated road centerlines, carriageways, junctions, footpaths, medians, parking areas, crossings and roadside assets using ArcGIS Pro and AutoCAD. Ensured compliance with PWA Roads & Drainage GIS Standards.

  • Drainage Network GIS Project (ASHGHAL, Qatar)
    Developed stormwater and foul sewer GIS datasets including manholes, pipes, gullies, catch pits, chambers and outfalls. Conducted topology validation, connectivity checks and GIS compliance verification.

  • FTTx & Optical Distribution Network (ODN) Project - Reliance Jio
    Fiber network planning, OLT design, feeder routes, trench alignment, manhole and handhole placement, fiber allocation, IFC cutsheets, BOM generation and GIS quality control.

  • LiDAR Power Transmission & Surface Mapping Project
    Processed airborne LiDAR datasets for power corridor mapping, surface modeling, DTM/DEM generation, contour creation, classified point clouds, orthophotos and transmission line analysis.

  • Land Information System (LUIS) Project - Australia
    Digitized buildings, road boundaries, sidewalks and vegetation from orthophotos and prepared topologically correct GIS datasets for national land information systems.

  • IndiCash ATM Network Planning Project
    Conducted GIS-based site suitability analysis and ATM location planning across multiple Indian states using Google Maps, Google Earth and ArcGIS.

  • Phoenix & WoNoBo Street View Mapping Project
    Performed Point of Interest (POI) mapping, 360-degree street-view integration, navigation data preparation, GIS attribution and quality assurance.

  • Noida Satellite Map Georeferencing Project
    Aligned AutoCAD parcel data with satellite imagery using ArcGIS georeferencing and spatial adjustment techniques to create accurate cadastral GIS datasets.

  • Land Use / Land Cover (LULC) Project - Africa
    Performed land cover classification using GeoEye satellite imagery, created AOI datasets, hydrology support layers and GIS databases for environmental analysis.

  • Survey & Civil 3D Projects
    Survey data processing, TIN surface generation, breakline creation, surface boundary management, terrain modeling, cut/fill volume calculations and earthwork analysis.
